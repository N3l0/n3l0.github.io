OCD

If you have OCD you’ll often have unwanted thoughts and fears. These unwanted thoughts and fears lead you to do the same things again and again, so called “compulsions“. This can cause significant problems in your everyday life. The anxiety increases if you try to stop or ignore the obsessions and so you’ll fall in the vicious cycle of OCD.

Symptoms

OCD normally includes symptoms of both obsessions and compulsion but you can also have only one of them. Obsessions can have multiple themes to them but these are the common ones:

  • Fear of contamination or dirt (like Aza has)
  • Needing things orderly and symmetrical
  • Doubting and having difficulty tolerating uncertainty

The symptoms of compulsion are usually connected to the obsessive thoughts and are behaviors that try to reduce your anxiety but they are only a temporary relief. Examples for compulsion are:

  • Washing and cleaning (like Aza has)
  • Orderliness
  • Checking
